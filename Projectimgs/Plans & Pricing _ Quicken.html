Frequently asked questions

What makes Quicken better than other personal finance apps?

Quicken has been trusted for over 40 years by 20+ million customers. With Quicken, you get a financial partner, for life — with everything you need, and the customization you want, all in one place. The depth and breadth of our features lead the industry. Many customers start out simply tracking their spending and end up creating intricately personalized investment systems to grow their net worth into the millions.

What are the differences between Quicken Simplifi and Quicken Classic?

For powerful, easy-to-use financial management, Quicken Simplifi is your go-to choice. It’s a cloud-based app designed for web & mobile. For more robust tools and in-depth features, our Quicken Classic line is a great option. It’s designed primarily for desktop use — on Windows or macOS — and comes with companion web & mobile apps.

What do I get with my Quicken subscription?

With your Quicken subscription, you always get the latest features as soon as they’re released. In addition, you also get:

  • The most up-to-date security for your data

  • Live customer support agents, available by phone or chat

  • Access to a vibrant subscriber community, where you can discuss & learn from other users

How does the 30-day, money-back guarantee work?

Within 30 days after purchasing or auto-renewing your subscription, you can request a refund here by filling out the form. You can read all the details here.

How do I know my data is secure?

We protect your data and transmit it securely from your financial institutions into Quicken with robust 256-bit encryption. If you want to learn more about all the ways your data is protected with us, click here.

Will Quicken sell my data?

We will never sell your data for any reason. The information downloaded from your banks is confidential and is used only to update your accounts. You can see the complete details of our privacy policy here.

Can I use Quicken products on my computer or mobile device?

Quicken Simplifi is available by mobile app & web app.  Quicken Classic is available on desktop, with companion web & mobile apps.

Can I use Quicken outside of the U.S.?

Quicken Simplifi is for the U.S. only. The Quicken Classic line is intended for subscribers in the U.S. and Canada, since we are only able to support banks from these regions at this time. For more information on Canadian products, please visit our Quicken Canada site.

Will I lose access to my data if I stop my subscription?

Quicken comes with a data access guarantee. If you ever decide to cancel your subscription, you can still access your past records in Quicken products. Click here to learn more.

What are the system requirements?

For Quicken Simplifi, all you need is an up-to-date browser or smartphone with internet access. For Quicken Windows, Quicken Mac, Quicken Mobile, and Quicken on the Web requirements, check out the Quicken System Requirements.

What if I need help getting set up?

Not a problem! Once you’ve purchased a Quicken subscription, you’re welcome to schedule a call with one of our dedicated agents. They’ll be happy to walk you through the product and get you all set up.
